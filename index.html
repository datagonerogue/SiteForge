About Me

Discover the Passion and Expertise Behind My Design Philosophy.

2010-2020.

From my teenage years, I've been captivated by the world of computers. I still remember configuring the control panel of Windows 7 on my old PC. The thrill of tweaking settings and understanding how the system worked fueled my curiosity.

Later, as I got a low end laptop from my family, a friend introduced me to the intriguing realm of Linux, starting with the powerful Kali Linux for delving into ethical hacking. I began experimenting with various Linux distributions, each offering a unique perspective on the power and flexibility of this operating system. The freedom to customize, tinker, and learn became the driving force behind my "distrohopping". From Ubuntu to Fedora, I expanded my knowledge with each installation.

2020-2022.

In the Pandemic Lockdown, I discovered the extent of control that big tech companies wield over the internet today. I made a conscious decision to prioritize my digital well-being. This led me to the world of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). I believe in the power of technology that respects user freedom, privacy, and transparency. Consequently, I've transitioned to using predominantly FOSS, finding it not only beneficial for my privacy but also more efficient in the day-to-day life.

As I deepened my understanding of the digital landscape, I naturally evolved into a digital rights advocate. I strongly believe in the right to privacy, digital freedom, and the importance of informed choices in the digital realm. Advocating for policies that protect these rights has become a cause close to my heart.

2022-Present.

Since joining my college, my exploration of the digital realm has taken a fascinating turn. After getting a powerful laptop, I ventured into the world of self-hosting and embraced the philosophy of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). On my older laptop, I've been running an ecosystem of FOSS projects, including Nextcloud for personal cloud services, Adguard Home for enhanced online security, and FreshRSS for a clutter-free news experience.

My deep dive into the world of FOSS led me to appreciate the philosophy of minimalism. I firmly believe in the power of simplicity, and it reflects not only in the software I choose, but also in the design of this website. Majorly crafted with basic HTML and CSS, this site mirrors my commitment to a streamlined and clutter-free digital experience. As I believe in delivering a clean and lightweight digital experience, by adhering to this minimalistic approach.
